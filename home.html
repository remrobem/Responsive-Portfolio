About Me

Rob Martin is an IT professional with 35 years of experience. Rob grew up as a developer in the IBM mainframe world, moved on to being an SAP developer, fell into web application vulnerability scanning and has plowed thru SAP security, governance, risk management and compliance.


Not one to shy away from change, Rob is now pursing challenges in web application development. Front-end, back-end, UI design, testing, anything is possible at this point.


Please don't hold this against him, but Rob has spent most of his career in a Fortune 250 company. Twenty six years of multi-million dollar projects, you're on your own projects, managing development and support teams, coding, testing, fixing, you name it.


Rob doesn't care much about moving up the management ladder, preferring to hone his creative and debugging skills in whatever platform is needed.


Rob and his wife Teresa have been married for 36 years. They raised 3 great sons, have a wonderful daughter-in-law and a fantastic grandson. And they have a cat they would rather not talk about.
